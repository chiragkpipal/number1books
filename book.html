The Book

The Healing Hands Book


See the book Learn More

Healing Hands is a valuable booklet which is designed for sick or injured children who are in hospitals across Canada. The book is filled with various activities designed to help the children pass the time and give them something fun to do while they are in the hospital. Filled with arts and crafts, puzzles, word games, short stories, jokes, and health and welfare information, the book will provide the children with hours of enjoyment and great information. The booklets are $5 per copy and valuable members of the business community can donate as many books as they would like to their local health care facility. It is only through this valuable support that make these books possible and appreciated so much by the children and their families.
